SURUCHI SANGHA



Suruchi Sangha entertains visitors with its arty outdoor display, which is usually themed on a different state of India each year. Although this Puja pandal is more than 50 years old, it first found fame in 2003 when it won an award for the best decorated pandal. The craftsmanship is exquisite.

Location: South Kolkata, in New Alipore near the petrol pump on Nalini Rajan Avenue. The nearest railways stations are Majerhat and Kalighat.

Famous for: Indian State Themes based Puja.
